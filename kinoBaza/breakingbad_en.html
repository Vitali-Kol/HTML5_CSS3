Breaking Bad

rating: 8.9 / 10 year: 2008 director: Adam Bernstein

Breaking Bad Description

High school chemistry teacher Walter White learns that he has lung cancer. Given the family's difficult financial situation and his limited prospects, Walter decides to start manufacturing methamphetamine. To do this, he enlists his former student Jesse Pinkman, who was once expelled from school with Walter's help. Pinkman was already involved in cooking meth but recently lost his partner and lab in a DEA raid.
