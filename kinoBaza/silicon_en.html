Silicon Valley

rating: 8.4 / 10 year: 2014 director: Mike Judge

Silicon Valley Description

The story of a group of geeks preparing to launch their own startups in the high-tech hub of San Francisco. The main characters of the series live for free in the house of a local millionaire, but in return, they have to give him 10% of the profits from future projects.
