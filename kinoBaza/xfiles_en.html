The X-Files

rating: 8.4 / 10 year: 2014 director: Mike Judge

The X-Files Description

FBI agents Dana Scully and Fox Mulder are assigned to work on the "X-Files" project. This is an archive of unsolved cases related to paranormal phenomena. Mulder believes in aliens and tries to convince the skeptic Scully that not everything can be explained rationally. Gradually, mutual distrust turns into friendship and even deeper feelings.
